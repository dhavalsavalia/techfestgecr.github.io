'Code Hunt' is an event as technical treasure hunt. Team use their brain power and coding skills to solve clues (riddles). In which the clues are based on code debugging in C language, which will leads them to final round and become a winner.
This is a team participant event.
The co-ordinators reserve the right to make any change to event whenever deemed.

Necessary:
Use of internet is prohibited.
No any electronic devices are permitted once the event starts.
Any sort of nuisance will not be tolerated and will result into disqualification.

Event Flow:

Round: 1 (Day 1, 4 hours)
In this round participants (teams) will be given set of riddles one by one whose outcome will be an alphabet pointed to any department of college. In this round, winner teams will be given a riddle whose outcome will reaches the team to any of the department of college there they will get another one which leads to another department and so on. Judgement Criteria:

Teams will advance to next round on the basis of completing the task and time taken for that. Team with lesser completion time will be winner in each round. If any team is not able to find the clue, they'll be eliminated.
