Details:

A zip line/River crossing consists of a pulley suspended on a cable, usually made of strong rope, mounted on a slope. It is designed to enable cargo or a person propelled by gravity to travel from the top to the bottom of the inclined cable/rope by holding on to or being attached to the freely moving pulley.This is also one of entertaining yet adventurous activity.It helps in moving from one place to other place quite easily and very fast without using much physical strength.
